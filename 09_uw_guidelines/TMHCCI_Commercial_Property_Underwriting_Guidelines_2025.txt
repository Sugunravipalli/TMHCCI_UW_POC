1. SCOPE AND APPLICATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
These guidelines apply to all Commercial Property risks underwritten by
Tokio Marine HCC International (TMHCCI) through Lloyd's Syndicate 4141.
They cover open market property business placed through Lloyd's brokers.

All underwriters must comply with these guidelines. Exceptions require
written referral to the appropriate authority level as defined in Section 8.

2. APPETITE STATEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

2.1 TARGET CLASSES (Actively Seeking)
   - Retail: Shopping centres, standalone retail, mixed-use retail
   - Manufacturing: Light and heavy manufacturing, assembly, processing
   - Office: Class A and B commercial office space
   - Warehouse & Distribution: Standard distribution, cold storage, logistics
   - Technology: Data centres, technology campuses, R&D facilities
   - Real Estate: Commercial portfolios, mixed-use developments
   - Food & Beverage: Processing, storage, restaurant groups
   - Healthcare: Hospitals, clinics, medical office buildings
   - Education: Universities, colleges, private schools
   - Hospitality: Hotels, resorts (subject to CAT restrictions in Section 4)

2.2 ACCEPTABLE CLASSES (Case-by-Case)
   - Energy: Storage facilities, renewable installations (requires SVP review)
   - Agricultural: Processing, storage, cooperatives
   - Pharmaceutical: Manufacturing, R&D, warehousing
   - Automotive: Manufacturing, dealership networks
   - Entertainment: Theatres, venues (excludes amusement parks)

2.3 EXCLUDED CLASSES (Do Not Quote)
   - Cannabis cultivation, processing, or dispensary
   - Fireworks manufacturing or storage
   - Mining operations (surface or underground)
   - Ammunition or explosives manufacturing
   - Waste processing or landfill operations
   - Nuclear facilities
   - Amusement parks or water parks
   - Aircraft hangars with fueling operations
   - Petrochemical refining (upstream)
   - Nightclubs or adult entertainment venues

3. RISK PARAMETERS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

3.1 TOTAL INSURED VALUE (TIV)
   - Minimum TIV per submission:     $5,000,000
   - Maximum TIV per risk:           $500,000,000
   - Single location TIV > $100M:    Requires SVP referral
   - Single location TIV > $250M:    Requires CUO approval

3.2 NUMBER OF LOCATIONS
   - No minimum or maximum location count
   - Portfolios > 50 locations: enhanced data requirements apply
   - Single location risks are acceptable

3.3 POLICY PERIOD
   - Standard: 12 months
   - Maximum: 18 months (requires SVP approval for > 12 months)
   - Short-period policies: Pro-rata minimum, subject to minimum premium

4. TERRITORY GUIDELINES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

4.1 ACCEPTABLE TERRITORIES (Standard Underwriting)
   - United States (all states, subject to CAT restrictions below)
   - Canada (all provinces)
   - United Kingdom
   - European Union / EEA member states
   - Australia and New Zealand

4.2 RESTRICTED TERRITORIES (Require Enhanced Review)
   - Florida: Tier 1 coastal counties (Miami-Dade, Broward, Palm Beach,
     Monroe, Collier, Lee) -- maximum 30% of TIV in Tier 1 FL
   - Texas Gulf Coast: Galveston, Cameron, Nueces, San Patricio,
     Aransas, Calhoun, Matagorda counties -- wind deductible required
   - California: Earthquake fault zones within 10 miles of known fault
   - Caribbean: All territories -- requires SVP approval, fac mandatory
   - Gulf Coast (LA, MS, AL coastal): Wind tier surcharge applies

4.3 EXCLUDED TERRITORIES
   - OFAC sanctioned countries (Iran, Syria, North Korea, Cuba, Crimea)
   - Active conflict zones
   - Countries without recognised legal framework for insurance claims

5. COVERAGE PARAMETERS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

5.1 STANDARD COVERAGE FORMS
   - ISO CP 10 30 (Causes of Loss - Special Form) or equivalent
   - Manuscript forms acceptable with Legal review

5.2 MINIMUM DEDUCTIBLES BY TIV BAND
   TIV Band                  Minimum Deductible
   -------------------------|-----------------------
   $5M - $25M               | $25,000
   $25M - $75M              | $50,000
   $75M - $150M             | $100,000
   $150M - $300M            | $250,000
   $300M - $500M            | $500,000

5.3 WIND/NAMED STORM DEDUCTIBLES (CAT-Exposed Territories)
   - Florida Tier 1:        5% of location TIV, minimum $250,000
   - Florida Tier 2:        3% of location TIV, minimum $100,000
   - Texas Gulf Coast:      3% of location TIV, minimum $100,000
   - Caribbean:             5% of location TIV, minimum $500,000
   - All other wind-exposed: 2% of location TIV, minimum $50,000

5.4 FLOOD SUBLIMITS
   - SFHA Zone A or V:      Maximum $5M sublimit per location
   - SFHA Zone B or X500:   Maximum $10M sublimit per location
   - Zone C or X:           Full limits available

5.5 EARTHQUAKE SUBLIMITS
   - Within 10mi of known fault:    Maximum $10M sublimit
   - California Zone 4:             3% deductible, $15M max sublimit
   - Pacific Northwest (WA/OR):     2% deductible, $20M max sublimit

6.2 MAXIMUM DEVIATION FROM TECHNICAL RATE
   - Without referral:    15% below technical (Levels 1 and 2)
   - With SVP approval:   20% below technical (Level 3)
   - With CUO approval:   25% below technical (Level 4)
   - Above technical:     No limit (above-technical pricing always acceptable)

6.3 RATE ADEQUACY REVIEW TRIGGERS
   Any of the following triggers a mandatory pricing review:
   - Deviation > 10% below technical rate
   - 5-year loss ratio > 50% on renewal business
   - Large loss (> $500K) within past 24 months
   - Competitive pressure cited as reason for rate reduction

6.4 MINIMUM PREMIUM
   - All risks: $15,000 minimum annual premium
   - Portfolios > 10 locations: $25,000 minimum annual premium

7. LOSS HISTORY REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

7.1 REQUIRED DOCUMENTATION
   - New Business: 5-year loss runs from current/prior carriers (mandatory)
   - Renewals: Current carrier loss runs updated to within 90 days
   - Large accounts (TIV > $100M): 10-year loss history preferred

7.2 LOSS RATIO THRESHOLDS
   - 5-year loss ratio < 30%:    Standard pricing applies
   - 5-year loss ratio 30-50%:   Enhanced review, rate increase expected
   - 5-year loss ratio 50-75%:   SVP review required, significant rate action
   - 5-year loss ratio > 75%:    CUO approval to renew, non-renewal recommended

7.3 LARGE LOSS REVIEW
   - Any single loss > $250,000:  Document in UW notes with root cause analysis
   - Any single loss > $500,000:  SVP notification required
   - Any single loss > $1,000,000: CUO notification, engineering inspection required

8. AUTHORITY MATRIX
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   See separate Authority Letter document for current authority levels.
   Summary:

   Level | Title         | Max Net Line | Max TIV    | Max Deviation
   ------|---------------|-------------|------------|---------------
   1     | Underwriter   | $5M         | $50M       | 5%
   2     | Senior UW     | $15M        | $150M      | 15%
   3     | SVP           | $25M        | $300M      | 20%
   4     | CUO           | $50M        | $500M      | 25%

   REFERRAL TRIGGERS (any single trigger requires referral):
   - TIV exceeds authority level maximum
   - Net line exceeds authority level maximum
   - Rate deviation exceeds authority level maximum
   - 5-year loss ratio > 50%
   - Restricted territory exposure > 30% of TIV
   - Excluded class exception request
   - New broker relationship (first submission)
   - Policy period > 12 months
   - Manuscript form or non-standard coverage

9. CONSTRUCTION AND PROTECTION STANDARDS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

9.1 PREFERRED CONSTRUCTION
   - Fire Resistive (ISO Class 6)
   - Non-Combustible (ISO Class 4-5)
   - Modified Fire Resistive

9.2 ACCEPTABLE CONSTRUCTION
   - Joisted Masonry (ISO Class 3) -- rate surcharge applies
   - Heavy Timber (ISO Class 2) -- rate surcharge applies, sprinkler required

9.3 RESTRICTED CONSTRUCTION
   - Wood Frame (ISO Class 1) -- maximum TIV $10M per building
   - Metal clad on wood frame -- surcharge applies

9.4 SPRINKLER REQUIREMENTS
   - TIV > $25M per building:      Automatic sprinkler system required
   - TIV > $50M per building:      Full sprinkler + central station alarm
   - Non-sprinklered buildings:     Maximum $10M per building TIV
   - Waiver of sprinkler requirement requires SVP approval with rate surcharge

9.5 PROTECTION CLASS
   - Protection Class 1-5:    Standard rating
   - Protection Class 6-7:    5% surcharge
   - Protection Class 8-9:    10% surcharge, maximum TIV $25M per building
   - Protection Class 10:     Decline (no public fire protection)

10. REINSURANCE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

10.1 NET RETENTION
   - Maximum net retention per risk:    $25,000,000
   - Maximum net retention per event:   $50,000,000
   - Any risk requiring gross line > $25M: Fac reinsurance mandatory

10.2 FACULTATIVE REINSURANCE
   - Required when gross line exceeds net retention
   - Required for all Caribbean risks
   - Required for any single location TIV > $100M
   - Fac must be placed and confirmed before binding

10.3 AGGREGATE MANAGEMENT
   - CAT aggregate limits monitored quarterly
   - Wind PML: Maximum $200M gross, $75M net (100-year return)
   - Earthquake PML: Maximum $100M gross, $40M net (250-year return)
   - Flood PML: Maximum $75M gross, $30M net (100-year return)

11. SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

11.1 MINIMUM INFORMATION TO QUOTE
   The following information is required before any indication can be provided:
   - Named insured and mailing address
   - Schedule of Values (SOV) with location-level detail
   - Occupancy description per location
   - Construction type per location
   - Year built per location
   - Sprinkler/protection details per location
   - Coverage limits requested (property damage + business interruption)
   - Deductible preference
   - Effective and expiry dates
   - 5-year loss history or loss runs
   - Current carrier and expiring premium (if renewal/remarketing)

11.2 PREFERRED ADDITIONAL INFORMATION
   - Engineering reports or inspection reports
   - Business interruption worksheet
   - Property condition reports
   - CAT model results (if available)
   - Financial statements (for BI validation)
   - Sprinkler inspection certificates

12. DOCUMENTATION STANDARDS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   All quoted risks must have documented UW notes including:
   - Risk assessment summary
   - Pricing rationale (especially if deviating from technical)
   - Comparable risks referenced
   - Claims analysis summary
   - Any conditions or warranties applied
   - Market context and competitive positioning
