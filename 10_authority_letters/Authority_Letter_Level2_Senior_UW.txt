BINDING AUTHORITY LIMITS
================================================================================

1. PER-RISK AUTHORITY
   - Maximum Net Line:              $15,000,000
   - Maximum Gross Line:            $30,000,000
   - Maximum Total Insured Value:   $150,000,000

2. PRICING AUTHORITY
   - Maximum Deviation from Technical Rate:    15% below technical
   - Above-technical pricing:                  Unlimited (no restriction)
   - Minimum premium:                          $15,000

3. TERRITORY
   - United States (all states, subject to CAT guidelines)
   - Canada
   - United Kingdom
   - EU / EEA
   - Australia / New Zealand

4. COVERAGE FORMS
   - Standard ISO forms
   - Pre-approved manuscript endorsements (per approved endorsement list)
   - Novel manuscript requires SVP review

5. POLICY PERIOD
   - Maximum 12 months
   - 18-month policies: SVP approval required

================================================================================
MANDATORY REFERRAL TRIGGERS
================================================================================

The Authority Holder MUST refer to Level 3 (SVP) when ANY of the following:

   [R1]  Total Insured Value exceeds $150,000,000
   [R2]  Net line exceeds $15,000,000
   [R3]  Rate deviation exceeds 15% below technical
   [R4]  5-year loss ratio exceeds 75%
   [R5]  Any single loss exceeds $1,000,000 in past 24 months
   [R6]  Caribbean territory
   [R7]  Excluded class exception requested
   [R8]  Energy sector (all risks regardless of size)
   [R9]  Aggregate PML contribution > $10M at 100-year return
   [R10] Policy period > 12 months
   [R11] Novel manuscript form
   [R12] Gross line requires > $30M fac placement
