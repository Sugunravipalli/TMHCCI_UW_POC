BINDING AUTHORITY LIMITS
================================================================================

1. PER-RISK AUTHORITY
   - Maximum Net Line:              $5,000,000
   - Maximum Gross Line:            $10,000,000
   - Maximum Total Insured Value:   $50,000,000

2. PRICING AUTHORITY
   - Maximum Deviation from Technical Rate:    5% below technical
   - Above-technical pricing:                  Unlimited (no restriction)
   - Minimum premium:                          $15,000

3. TERRITORY
   - United States (all states, subject to CAT guidelines)
   - Canada
   - United Kingdom
   - EU / EEA

4. COVERAGE FORMS
   - Standard ISO forms only
   - No manuscript endorsements without Senior UW review

5. POLICY PERIOD
   - Maximum 12 months

================================================================================
MANDATORY REFERRAL TRIGGERS
================================================================================

The Authority Holder MUST refer to the next authority level (Level 2 -
Senior Underwriter) when ANY of the following conditions apply:

   [R1]  Total Insured Value exceeds $50,000,000
   [R2]  Net line exceeds $5,000,000
   [R3]  Rate deviation exceeds 5% below technical
   [R4]  5-year loss ratio exceeds 50%
   [R5]  Any single loss exceeds $250,000 in past 24 months
   [R6]  Risk is in restricted territory (FL Tier 1, TX Gulf, Caribbean)
   [R7]  Occupancy is in "Acceptable - Case by Case" category
   [R8]  Excluded class exception is requested
   [R9]  New broker relationship (first submission from broker)
   [R10] Policy period exceeds 12 months
   [R11] Manuscript form or non-standard endorsement
   [R12] Facultative reinsurance required
   [R13] Construction is Wood Frame or Metal Clad
   [R14] Building is unsprinklered with TIV > $10,000,000

================================================================================
DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

For all risks bound under this authority:
   - Complete UW notes in rating data sheet (mandatory)
   - Pricing rationale documented (mandatory)
   - Loss history reviewed and summarized (mandatory)
   - SOV verified against application (mandatory)
   - Comparable risks referenced where available (preferred)
